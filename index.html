Biography

Lei Kang is a lecturer of Computer Science at Shantou University. He received a PhD in Computer Science from Pattern Recognition and Document Analysis Group at Computer Vision Center (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona) and research group at omni:us in 2020, which was awarded with an Excellent Cum Laude (highest grade) qualification with Industrial mention. He was advised by Marçal Rusiñol, Alicia Fornés and Mauricio Villegas during the PhD. His research topics are based on handwritten document analysis, specifically, sequence-to-sequence and transformer based approaches for handwriting recognition, multi-writer adaptation, language modelling, GANs-based handwriting generation, etc.
