<h2>Biography</h2>
<p>Lei Kang is a lecturer of Computer Science at <a href="http://english.stu.edu.cn" target="_blank">Shantou University</a>. He received a PhD in Computer Science from <a href="http://dag.cvc.uab.es/" target="_blank">Pattern Recognition and Document Analysis Group</a> at <a href="http://www.cvc.uab.es/" target="_blank">Computer Vision Center (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona)</a> and research group at <a href="https://omnius.com/" target="_blank">omni:us</a> in 2020, which was awarded with an Excellent Cum Laude (highest grade) qualification with Industrial mention. He was advised by <a href="http://www.cvc.uab.es/~marcal/" target="_blank">Marçal Rusiñol</a>, <a href="http://www.cvc.uab.es/~afornes/" target="_blank">Alicia Fornés</a> and <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=18e7rpsAAAAJ" target="_blank">Mauricio Villegas</a> during the PhD. His research topics are based on handwritten document analysis, specifically, sequence-to-sequence and transformer based approaches for handwriting recognition, multi-writer adaptation, language modelling, GANs-based handwriting generation, etc.</p>
